Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes! We cater to various dietary needs including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and halal options. Please inform us of any allergies or restrictions when making your reservation.

Is there parking available?

Free street parking is available around the restaurant. We also have a secure parking garage 50 meters away for your convenience.

Do you offer takeaway and delivery?

Yes, we offer takeaway services. Delivery is available through Uber Eats and Mr D Food within a 10km radius of our restaurant.

Can I bring my own wine?

We have an extensive selection of South African wines, but we do allow BYO wine with a corkage fee of R50 per bottle.

Do you host cultural entertainment?

Yes! We feature traditional Zulu music and dance performances on Friday and Saturday evenings. Check our events calendar for specific dates.

Are children welcome?

Absolutely! We're a family-friendly restaurant with high chairs available and a special children's menu featuring mild, kid-friendly versions of traditional dishes.
