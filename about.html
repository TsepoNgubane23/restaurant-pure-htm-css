About Zungu Restaurant

A celebration of South African heritage through authentic cuisine

Our Story

Founded in 1998 by the Zungu family, our restaurant began as a small eatery in the heart of Durban, serving traditional Zulu cuisine to the local community. What started as Mama Nomsa Zungu's dream to share her grandmother's recipes has grown into one of Durban's most beloved dining destinations.

The name "Zungu" carries deep meaning in Zulu culture, representing strength, community, and the preservation of tradition. Our restaurant embodies these values, creating a space where families gather, stories are shared, and the rich culinary heritage of KwaZulu-Natal is celebrated.

Today, under the leadership of Chef Sipho Zungu (Mama Nomsa's son), we continue to honor our roots while embracing innovation. Our kitchen combines time-honored cooking methods passed down through generations with contemporary techniques, creating dishes that tell the story of South Africa's diverse culinary landscape.

Located in the vibrant Florida Road area of Morningside, Zungu Restaurant has become a cultural hub where locals and visitors alike come to experience authentic South African hospitality. Meet Our Team

S.M Zungu - 22337784

Executive Chef & Owner

Born and raised in Durban, Chef Sipho learned traditional Zulu cooking from his grandmother and mother. After training at the Durban University of Technology's culinary program and working in top restaurants across South Africa, he returned home to carry on the family legacy. His passion lies in preserving authentic flavors while presenting them in innovative ways.

Our Commitment to Excellence

Quality Ingredients

We source only the finest ingredients from trusted local suppliers. Our meat comes from free-range farms, our vegetables are organic when possible, and our spices are freshly ground daily.

Community Support

We actively support local farmers, employ from our community, and participate in local food security initiatives. A portion of our profits goes to feeding schemes in Durban townships.

Environmental Responsibility

We minimize waste through composting, use eco-friendly packaging, and have implemented energy-saving measures throughout our restaurant operations.

Skills Development

We provide training and development opportunities for our staff, including culinary apprenticeships and hospitality skills programs, contributing to the growth of South Africa's tourism industry.
